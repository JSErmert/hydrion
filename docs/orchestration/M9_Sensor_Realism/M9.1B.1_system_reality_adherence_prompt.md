## Required Classification System

For every subsystem and key component, classify:

- `source-grounded` — directly supported by literature or known physics
- `derived-grounded` — logically derived from grounded physics but not directly cited
- `internal assumption` — chosen design decision without external grounding
- `placeholder` — explicitly temporary or unvalidated parameterization
- `unknown` — insufficient information to classify
