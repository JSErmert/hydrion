## 7. Four-Scenario Classification

**Scenario: v2 approximately equals or exceeds v1 (v2 ≥ v1)**

Condition met: v2 mean return (841.839) ≥ v1 mean return (694.639). Delta = +21.2%.

**Binding interpretation (M8.3 Section 9):**
obs8_deployment_v1 achieves near-equivalent or better in-simulation performance than
obs14_v1 under these training conditions. The policy is not penalized — and is measurably
aided — by operating on 8 deployment-realistic channels rather than 14.

**Must NOT conclude:**
- "deployment-ready" — M8 does not test hardware, calibration, or real-world conditions
- "deployment gap solved" — result is confounded by placeholder sensor noise and the
  360k-step (not 500k-step) training budget; a negative gap is not structurally impossible
  at different step counts or after sensor calibration

**Interpretive note on the positive gap:**
The +21.2% result is consistent with multiple non-exclusive explanations: (a) a lower-
dimensional observation space may present a simpler optimization landscape for PPO at this
budget; (b) the placeholder sensor noise in obs8 channels 4–7 may be providing a form of
implicit regularization under VecNormalize; (c) the 360k-step run is not directly comparable
to the 500k-step v1 run. None of these explanations can be confirmed without additional
ablation. This result establishes that no large negative deployment gap exists at 360k steps
under current noise parameters — it does not establish deployment viability.
