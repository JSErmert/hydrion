## 7. Prohibited-Claims Compliance

The following claims are explicitly prohibited by M8.3 Section 10, regardless of evaluation outcome:

| Prohibited claim | Why prohibited |
|-----------------|----------------|
| "deployment-ready" | M8 does not test hardware, calibration, or real-world conditions |
| "channels 0–5 are unnecessary" | The comparison is bounded by placeholder noise; confound not yet resolved |
| "obs8_deployment_v1 is sensor-only control" | Indices 0–3 are actuator feedback, not sensor readings |
| "deployment is impossible" | A gap may reflect training budget or noise quality, not fundamental insufficiency |
| "v2 is better than v1" | The comparison is deployment-gap measurement, not a quality ordering |
| "M8 proves deployment viability" | Single-seed, placeholder-noise-bounded result does not establish viability |

The eval script prints these prohibited claims in its output header to prevent misinterpretation
during reporting.
