### Important limitations to carry forward into M8+

1. **Not deployment-ready.** Truth channels 0–9 (indices 0–9 in obs14_v1) are privileged
   simulation-only inputs unavailable at hardware deployment. The trained policy depends on
   these channels. Strong eval performance does NOT prove sensor-only control sufficiency.

2. **Not cross-comparable to ppo_cce_v2.** Different environment (HydrionEnv vs CCE),
   different physics, different reward, different observation schema. Performance numbers
   are not interchangeable.

3. **Calibration-pending noise parameters.** `dp_drift_rate`, `dp_fouling_gain`, and
   `flow_calibration_bias_std` are placeholder values. obs14_v1 policy behavior will change
   when calibration replaces these placeholders. Domain randomization of sensor params is
   deferred.

4. **Episode repetition in deterministic eval.** Episodes 01–04 in PPO eval produced
   identical returns (708.498). This is expected behavior under a deterministic policy with
   a deterministic environment — it does not indicate an evaluation bug, but it does limit
   variance estimates from small episode counts.
